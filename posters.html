HIGH RES. DIGITAL POSTERS
Ideal for short runs

Stretford Studios offer fantastic value full colour digital posters. Ideal for short runs and high impact point of sale or advertising.
We print on many different grades of paper with a variety of finishes. Our 210gsm Semi-gloss posters prove extremely popular and are available at excellent prices. Please consult the chart below for examples on price.
We will even help with the artwork if required.

SCREENPRINTED POSTERS
& Billboards

Stretford Studios can provide high quality in-house screenprinted posters and billboards at very competitive prices.
Screen Printed posters represent the best way to make a BIG impact and we regularly supply major high street retailers with screen printed 'Sale' posters.
Screenprinting can prove to be an excellent alternative to digital printing and, at a fraction of the cost.

LITHO-PRINT POSTERS
Ideal for large runs

Looking for full colour posters to plaster your message all over the place? Don't need the high resolution output that digital printing provides?
Look no further than our Litho Print Posters.
From A3 to A1 and quantities over 250, these posters are ideal.
Available in a number of finishes on differing paper weights and optional laminating.
Please feel free to give us a call to discuss how Litho Printed Posters can help you.
